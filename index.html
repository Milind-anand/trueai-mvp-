About TrueAI

TrueAI is a free AI content detection tool for journalists, educators, content creators, and curious readers.

⚠ Accuracy Disclaimer & Limitation of Liability
TrueAI's basic (free) detection mode is approximately 60–65% accurate. The Pro mode (powered by Claude AI) achieves approximately 80–85% accuracy. No AI detection tool is 100% accurate.

TrueAI does not guarantee the accuracy of any result. Results should be treated as informational indicators only — not as definitive proof that any content is or is not AI-generated.

We explicitly disclaim all liability for any loss, damage, legal action, disciplinary action, reputational harm, or any other consequence arising directly or indirectly from the use of TrueAI results. Users should exercise their own judgment and seek professional advice before acting on any detection result.

TrueAI is not responsible for false positives (human content flagged as AI) or false negatives (AI content flagged as human). Human-written content may sometimes score high; AI content that has been edited may score low.

For higher accuracy, upgrade to Pro. Pro mode uses Claude (Anthropic's AI) for semantic-level analysis with significantly improved reliability.
How it works
Free mode analyzes statistical patterns — sentence uniformity, vocabulary diversity, structural regularity, and AI-typical buzzwords. Pro mode uses Claude (Anthropic's AI) for deeper semantic analysis. Neither mode stores your content after analysis.
Privacy policy
  • We do not store any text you paste into TrueAI
  • No account required for free tier
  • API keys stored only in your browser's local storage — never on our servers
  • We do not sell or share any user data
  • Analytics collect only anonymous page view counts
  • Pro mode text is sent to Anthropic's API under their privacy policy
  • Scan history is stored locally in your browser only
AdSense & advertising
TrueAI displays advertisements via Google AdSense to fund free access. Ads are served by Google and are subject to Google's advertising policies. TrueAI does not control ad content. If you see an inappropriate ad, please use the AdChoices button on the ad to report it.
