Our Science

How VeriAI detects AI content

VeriAI uses a multi-signal detection approach powered by Gemini AI — analyzing the same linguistic patterns that distinguish AI writing from human writing, across text, web pages and videos.

📊
Perplexity Analysis
AI models always pick the statistically most likely next word. This makes AI text highly predictable. VeriAI measures how "surprising" each word choice is — low perplexity means AI, high perplexity means human.
📈
Burstiness Score
Humans write in bursts — mixing short punchy sentences with long complex ones. AI writes uniformly. VeriAI measures this variation (burstiness). Low burstiness is a strong AI signal.
🔤
AI Vocabulary Fingerprint
AI models overuse specific words — "furthermore", "leverage", "delve", "multifaceted", "paramount", "transformative". VeriAI has a trained dictionary of 200+ AI-typical phrases.
🧠
Structural Pattern Detection
AI writes in perfect paragraph blocks — always 3-5 sentences, always balanced. Humans ramble, repeat differently, go off-topic. VeriAI detects this structural uniformity.
🫂
Human Authenticity Markers
Real human writing contains typos, self-corrections, colloquialisms, hedging language ("I think", "maybe"), personal anecdotes, and specific details only a human would know.
🔍
Sentence-Level Classification
VeriAI doesn't just give a single score — it classifies each sentence individually and highlights the specific sentences most likely written by AI.
